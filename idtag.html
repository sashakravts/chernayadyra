Graphic design raises a lot of questions and opens up space for the debates. Commercial design is outdated and we need to be brave enough to form a new concept around it, have an ability to choose for ourselves what we want to do.
My views on it are close to what Experimental Jetset talks about “ design being a platform for creativity, authorship and self-expression”.
Furthermore, the surroundings and current state of it, appreciation of the history and understanding of where and what the world is now and what it can possibly be - this is what guides me in my work.
