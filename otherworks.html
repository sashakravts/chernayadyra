During the first year I mainly relied on the base that I had before - school. For instance, my biggest inspiration and influence (in my younger times) was Anselm Kiefer. By looking at his work I unconsciously became open to large works and installations, realising it only now, by looking back. I was not afraid of going out and creating a piece with the materials I could find on the street. It was all about the idea at that time and as long as it was there, I could easily spend hours, preparing for what I want to do. “Empty places” project was one of them. It was the longest journey so far, in terms of how long the idea was carried out, transitioned and evaluated.
